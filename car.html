A car is a road vehicle used for transportation. It usually has four wheels, runs on petrol, diesel, or electricity, and is designed to carry people from one place to another safely and quickly.

  • The first car was invented by Karl Benz in 1886, called the Benz Patent Motorwagen.
  • In 1908, Ford Model T made cars affordable for common people.
  • Today, cars are more advanced with smart features, sensors, and electric engines.
